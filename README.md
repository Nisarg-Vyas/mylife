# ✦ MyLife — College & Life Tracker

A personal tracker for students. Log your semester, habits, diary, and expenses — all synced to the cloud.

**🌐 Live:** [Nisarg-Vyas.github.io/mylife](https://Nisarg-Vyas.github.io/mylife)  
**📖 Full Guide:** [Read the detailed guide →](./GUIDE.md)

---

## What's inside

| Tab | What it does |
|-----|-------------|
| 🎓 **College** | Semester setup, courses, exams, vacation tracker, full calendar with tithis & festivals |
| 📊 **Track** | Daily habit logger — track count, clean vs. with "it", monthly grid view |
| 📔 **Diary** | Daily journal with milestones, mood, expense log, and day score |
| ✨ **Insights** | Streaks, avg score, mood chart, spending total, 30-day bar chart |

---

## Quick Start

1. Open the website link above
2. Click **Create Account** → enter name, email, password
3. Go to **🎓 College** → click **Start Setup** → fill in your semester details
4. Go to **📔 Diary** → click **⚙️ Edit Milestones** → add your daily goals
5. Go to **⚙️ BTech Settings** (avatar icon, top right) → enter your start & end dates

That's it — you're set up. Everything saves to the cloud automatically.

---

## Features at a glance

- 📅 Full semester calendar with Indian festivals, tithis, exam & vacation highlights
- 🎓 BTech countdown — days, months, years left until graduation
- 📊 Habit tracker with clean/with-it split and monthly totals
- 📔 Daily diary — milestones (digital & analogue), expenses (₹), journal, mood
- ✨ Streak counter, day score chart, mood distribution
- ☁️ Cloud sync via Firebase — works on any device
- 🔐 Email/password login or Google sign-in
